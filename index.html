Online text encryptro and decryptor. The site doesn't store your passwords or texts. There's no backend here — just frontend that doesn't remember anything. Be careful: we really don't know your password and can't repair it.

How to use:

  1. Type your secret text in Decrypted area.
  2. Enter your password.
  3. Press the Encrypt button.
  4. Copy-past Encrypted text to your cloud storage like Google Drive Doc.
